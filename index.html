Who We Are

A Movement
Rooted in Community.

Tulizo Foundation was born in the highlands of Iten, Elgeyo Marakwet – the world-renowned "Home of Champions." We exist to ensure that the same spirit of resilience and excellence that makes Iten famous also reaches every woman and girl in our community.

We are here to make sure the strength and resilience of Iten's athletes belong to our women and girls, too. We tackle gender-based violence and inequality on the ground – using sport, mentorship, and real community action.
